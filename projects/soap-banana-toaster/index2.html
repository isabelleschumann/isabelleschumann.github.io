About Me

Hello there! I’m Isabelle Schumann, Also known on social media as: Soap Banana Toaster. (Soap.)

My background and where I come from:

I was Born in the state of Michigan. At 8 years old I moved to the state of Florida in 2008.My parents were divorced and my family was broken. I survived a broken household.My start in life was NEVER easy. And there were times as a kid I wished it was.Bullies, and my own father would say that I would "never make it in this world as an artist."

But I'm here today to prove them wrong, and to stand up for fellow artists, to make a difference in this world. I am here to INSPIRE artists like you, to KEEP GOING, and to make a living and lifestyle out of creating what you love. Art.

I kept going because I believed in myself, and SO CAN YOU!

My goals and plans for the future:

For the future, I plan on becoming recognized for my work in Graphic, and UX/UI design. As well as among anything Digital Art related.I have many platforms where I post my work. (Instagram, NewGrounds, etc.)
