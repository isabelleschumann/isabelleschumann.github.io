History of Digital Art

When was Digital Art Invented?

The earliest forms of digital art can be traced back to the 1960s, when artists began experimenting with computers as a medium for creating visual art. One of the first artists to work with digital technology was John Whitney, who used a computer to create abstract animations in the 1960s.

when was Clip Studio Paint made?

CLIP STUDIO PAINT was released in 2012. CLIP STUDIO PAINT is an all-purpose drawing tool where users can draw both comics and illustrations.
